ABOUT US

The focus of the "Student Tracking and Attendance via RFID using Google Sheets" project is to streamline and modernize student attendance tracking by utilizing RFID technology to eliminate the need for traditional paper-based systems, thereby enhancing efficiency and accuracy in attendance management. This aids the educational aspect of our school- Batangas State University - Alangilan Campus

Efficiency and Reliability

Implementing an RFID and cloud-based system can revolutionize attendance management by streamlining processes and ensuring real-time monitoring. RFID technology offers automated, error-free tracking, while cloud solutions provide secure, accessible data storage. Together, they enhance efficiency, reliability, and overall productivity.

Real-time Data

Utilize IoT devices paired with Google Sheets for seamless, automated attendance updates in real-time. This integration ensures instant data capture and accessibility from any device, anywhere. It's a powerful combination for efficient and reliable attendance management.

Enhanced User Experience

Implement intuitive interfaces alongside visual confirmations to enhance the user experience during attendance scans. This approach ensures clarity and ease of use, minimizing errors and confusion. Visual feedback, such as green checkmarks or confirmation messages, provides immediate assurance of successful scans, making the system user-friendly and efficient./p>

PROCEDURE

LIMIT IT TO 2 SENTENCES The development and implementation of the RFID-based attendance system follow a structured methodology, starting with the planning phase and gathering essential components. This is followed by setting up Google Apps Script, deploying it as a web app, and conducting rigorous testing to ensure seamless integration with Google Sheets for accurate attendance tracking

Hardware Assembly

"First, gather components including the ESP8266, RFID reader, LCD, and buzzer; then connect the RFID reader to the ESP8266 pins, and finally, wire the LCD and buzzer according to the schematic."

Google Sheets Setup

"Start by creating a new spreadsheet for attendance, then set up a Google Apps Script, and finally deploy it as a web app to get the URL.

Programming

"Upload the code to the ESP8266, test the RFID scanning, configure the WiFi credentials and deployment URL, and finally, confirm that the data appears in Google Sheets
